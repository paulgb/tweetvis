Twitter Conversation Visualisation

THIS IS OBSOLTETE! Have a look at Treeverse instead.

Conversations on Twitter have lives of their own; one tweet can elicit many replies, each of which may itself have many replies. Tracing the structure of these conversations in a tree-like visualisation is useful for understanding the structure of these conversations, as well as following along with complex discussion threads.

The visualization is implemented as a bookmarklet for Google Chrome. To use it, drag the button below to your Bookmarks toolbar (you may have to make the bookmarks bar visible from the View menu first).
